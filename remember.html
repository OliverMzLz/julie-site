If You Ever Forgot...

💭 If you ever forgot how brave you are
Just remember all the things you've gotten through without ever needing to tell the world how hard it was.
🌙 If you ever forgot how beautiful your silence is
I’ve always found peace in your pauses and comfort in your silence.
🫶 If you ever forgot how much you matter
Know that I waited half a year for you, and would have waited years if it meant, I finally hear from you.
🌸 If you ever forgot your worth
Know that its okay, because I don't think anything could ever equate to the worth that you have to me or the world.
💌 If you ever forgot that you are missed.
I hope you remember, I used to listen to your playlist you made me all the time, and would constantly re-read our old text.
🦋 If you ever forgot how much you mean to me
When you were gone, I spoke to God about you, and only hoped that you were okay.
🎠 If you ever forgot how much joy you bring
Remember that even your smallest laugh changed my whole day.
💬 If you ever forgot that you don’t need to be perfect
Remember: I never wanted perfect, I just wanted you.
🧩 If you ever forgot that someone understands you
Know that I always try to listen between the lines, even when you didn’t say much.
🔐 If you ever forgot you’re safe with me
I never needed your secrets, just your comfort in knowing I would never leave or abandon you.
